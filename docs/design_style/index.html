ORIGINS & PHILOSOPHY

Cyberpunk is a subgenre of science fiction in a dystopian futuristic setting that tends to focus on a "combination of low-life and high tech". It features advanced technological and scientific achievements, such as artificial intelligence and cybernetics, juxtaposed with a degree of breakdown or radical change in the social order.

Visually, it is defined by neon lights, dark cityscapes, Japanese typography, and a clash between the organic and the synthetic.

STYLE: KITSCH

STYLE OVER SUBSTANCE

The Golden Age of Excess

Kitsch is the main style employed in the Cyberpunk 2077 interface. It represents a bold, colorful visual experience.

  • Rounded Edges
  • Bright, Saturated Colors (Pink, Cyan, Yellow)
  • Minimal Flat Design with Gradients
  • Accessible and Pop-Culture Driven

STYLE: NEO-MILITARISM

SUBSTANCE OVER STYLE

Corporate Authority

Used by organizations like Militech and NCPD. This style reflects a corporate and serious intention, emphasizing authority and power.

  • Straight, Sharp Shapes
  • Dark, Muted Colors (Black, Red, Gunmetal)
  • Lack of Playfulness
  • Functional and Intimidating
